The Heart of True Biryani

Crafted with Patience. Served with Passion.

At Jai Bhavani Biriyani Point, we believe that great biryani is more than just a meal—it is a craft. Located in the vibrant heart of Kuvempu Nagar, we have dedicated ourselves to mastering the art of slow-cooked, aromatic rice and perfectly marinated, succulent meats.

We source only the finest spices and freshest local ingredients to ensure that every plate we serve carries the authentic, bold flavors you crave. Whether you are a student looking for a satisfying break, a family hosting a dinner, or a food lover seeking the best in Davanagere, we invite you to experience a standard of quality that turns a quick visit into a memorable culinary moment.

We aren’t just serving food; we are serving the tradition of excellence.
